Vowel Tuner Privacy Policy

Vowel Tuner is a student project realized within the scope of the second year of the MSc master in Natural Language Processing at the Institut du Digital, Management et Congition, a component of the Université de Lorraine. This privacy policy will explain how we use the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

What data do we collect?
Our Company collects the following data:
  • Gender information (provided in the form)
  • Audio files recorded by your microphone

How do we collect your data?
You directly provide Our Service with the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you click the "Rate my vowel!" button. If you recorded several audio files in a row, only the last one will be sent when clicking the button.

How will we use your data?
Our Service collects your data so that we can estimate the probability of you pronouncing a vowel correctly in a given recording.

How do we store your data?
Your data is processed and stored in a server located in the European Union. Your gender information is only kept in working memory ("RAM") and is never saved. Your audio recordings are saved temporarily for processing purposes, and the corresponding files are deleted immediately after the vowel rating is generated. In the unlikely event where the server would crash before your audio recordings have been deleted, they will be deleted upon rebooting.

What are your data protection rights?
We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:
  • The right to access – You have the right to request Our Company for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that Our Company correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Our Company to complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure – You have the right to request that Our Company erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that Our Company restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to Our Company’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability – You have the right to request that Our Company transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
